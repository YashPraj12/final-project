Our Story

Mom's Bakery

"Since 1995"

From our humble beginnings baking
in our home kitchen, we have grown into
a retail bakery that has been proudly
serving our specialities in Barrie and
surrounding areas since 1995. Come and visit

our store to try our freshly baked donuts,
cookies , muffins and native delicacies.
Our bakery is open from 8 am to 9pm.
We would be happy to serve you.
